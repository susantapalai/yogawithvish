The Philosophy

Yoga as a living practice, not a performance.

Vishakha — known warmly to her students as Vish — believes yoga was never meant to belong only to the flexible, the disciplined, or the spiritually inclined. It belongs to anyone willing to show up.

Based at Suncity Gloria on Sarjapur Main Road, Bengaluru, her studio has become a quiet refuge for professionals managing chronic stress, students navigating anxiety, and individuals seeking real healing — not just physical fitness.

Her approach is unhurried, deeply attentive, and rooted in the conviction that transformation happens when you stop performing and start listening — to your breath, your body, and your own innate wisdom.

I've never done yoga before. Is this right for me?

+

Absolutely. Many of Vishakha's most transformed students arrived with no prior experience whatsoever. She meets each person exactly where they are — no flexibility, fitness level, or background required. The first session is always an introduction, never a test.

I have a health condition. Can I still attend?

+

Yes — and you're particularly welcome. Therapeutic work is a core strength of Vishakha's practice. Students have found relief from migraines, chronic back pain, anxiety, stress, and postural issues. Simply share your history during the discovery call and the practice will be shaped accordingly.

What's the difference between private and group sessions?

+

Private sessions offer fully personalised instruction with no compromise — sequences are built entirely around your needs. Group classes offer community energy and are kept small (≤8 students) so you still receive genuine individual attention. Many students do both.

Where are you located and what are the timings?

+

The studio is located inside Suncity Gloria Apartments, Sarjapur Main Road, opposite Decathlon, Carmelaram, Bengaluru. Morning sessions open at 7:30 AM on weekdays. For current schedule and weekend availability, please call or send a message.

How long before I see results?

+

Most students notice something within the first two to three sessions — usually a sense of calm and improved breath awareness. Physical changes such as increased flexibility, reduced pain, and better posture typically emerge within 4–6 weeks of consistent practice. Lasting transformation is a few months in the making — and worth every session.
